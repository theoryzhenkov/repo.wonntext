Winfree oscillatory neural networks model computation through the collective
evolution of phase oscillators on a toroidal state space. Each oscillator has a
phase and a frequency.  Couplings between oscillators drive phases toward
synchrony.  In this text denoising task, discrete symbols are embedded as
frequencies, random phases are initialised, and bidirectional attention supplies
the coupling field.  A masked token is just another vocabulary entry; the model
must recover the original symbol from the final phase at every position.
